# FAQ

## Do we have to replace our current ERP?

No. Agentee offers two options: **Option A** (full Agentee with built-in chat)
or **Option B** (keep your current ERP and add Agentee Chat via API). Option B
works with systems including SAP, Odoo, and MISA.

## Do we need to migrate our data?

No. Agentee connects through APIs; existing data stays intact.

## Does the AI act on its own?

No. Agentee prepares actions (orders, POs, emails) and a user approves before
anything is committed or sent.

## What if the AI misunderstands a request?

Users can open the traditional ERP interface to check or edit data directly.

## What channels are supported?

Web UI and Telegram.

## Do new employees need training?

Agentee is designed for zero training — staff message in natural language and
can start from day one.
