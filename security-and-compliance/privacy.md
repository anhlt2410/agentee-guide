# Privacy

Agentee processes personal and company contact data, and — when outreach and
enrichment are enabled — shares some of it with third parties.

## External data sharing

* **Apollo.io enrichment** — lead/company data is sent to a third-party provider to retrieve contact details.
* **Gmail** — OAuth grants Agentee scoped access to send from the rep's mailbox.
