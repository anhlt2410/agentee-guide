# Access Control Model

Access is granted through **SSO from the ERP login portal** and enforced by
**role-based scoping** (see [Roles & Permissions](../admin/roles-and-permissions.md)).
Agentee inherits ERP identity and permissions rather than maintaining a separate
access system.
